Open your browser’s JavaScript console (right now, on this page) and set app.message to a different value.

For Google Chrome, here are the steps to do that:

  1. Open up the triple-dot "Customize and Control Google Chrome" menu (top right)
  2. Select "More Tools"
  3. Select "Developer Tools"
  4. Click on the Console tab
  5. In the console, type app.message = "<different value>"
